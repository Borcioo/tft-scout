### Observations

1. **Fix 1E is the biggest win.** `phaseCompanionSeeded` went from
   the dominant hot spot (~24 s cumulative) to a negligible contributor
   (~400 ms cumulative, −98%). The anchor-driven ranker replaced
   ~1 280 `buildOneTeam` calls with 30 targeted ones, and the locked
   scenarios went from 12 s per run to ~120 ms per run.

2. **Fix 2A delivered its expected cut.** `phaseTemperatureSweep`
   dropped from 12 s cumulative to 560 ms (all in the non-lock
   scenario; the skip-on-locked short-circuit takes it to ~0 ms on
   both locked runs — visible as `0.00` in the tables below).

3. **Fix 7 works.** TopN contract holds on all three scenarios plus
   the impossible case.

4. **No-lock still misses target B by 201 ms.** The non-lock run has
   no single dominant phase — `deepVertical` (563 ms), `temperatureSweep`
   (560 ms), `traitSeeded` (269 ms) and `companionSeeded` (156 ms)
   all contribute. Trimming any one further would need a separate
   tuning pass. The locked scenarios crush their targets and the
   overall perf story is a massive win; whether to iterate on non-lock
   or ship-and-tune-later is a controller decision.
